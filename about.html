ABOUT US

The Owners

Aletha Robertson & Joanna Davis are co-founders of Belle & Bay The Hair Company.

As Belle & Bay opened its doors in April 2021, Aletha & Joanna had the same vision to create a modern, high quality, service-focused salon that delivers the highest standard of hairdressing, with a focus on attentive and professional service.

Together they bring a wealth of industry knowledge and professional expertise to Tring, after gathering many years of experience from working across the globe and onboard the world's most renowned luxury cruise lines and private mega yachts. With their portfolio extending further to include photoshoots, fashion shows, weddings & events.
